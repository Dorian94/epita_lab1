Patato Mash

    I. Buy 2 pounds of Potatoes

    II. Peel 2 pounds of Potatoes

    III. Bring water to the boil

      1. Throw water in a pot.

      2. Put pot on cooker

      3. Switch cooker on

        a. Switch on the right plate

      4. Wait till it boils

    IV. Boil them

    V. Mash them up when they are soft
